Ranking of Renewable Energy Sources in Australia Over Time (1999-2023)

This bump chart tracks the relative rankings of various renewable energy sources (bioenergy, hydro, solar, wind) from 1999 to 2023. Hover over the lines to see more information about each energy source and its ranking over time.

Per Capita Energy Consumption by Australian State in 2024

This map visualizes the per capita energy consumption (in Petajoules) across Australian states. Darker shades indicate higher energy consumption per capita, giving a geographical perspective of energy usage.

Proportion of Fuel Production by Type in Australia (1983-2023)

This stacked area chart shows the proportion of each fuel type in Australia's energy production over time. Although renewable energy production has increased by 170% since 1983, the overall share of renewable energy has remained relatively stagnant.
